Scoring Scale

For each criterion:

Key Responsibilities:

  • Finalize award categories and criteria
  • Design and distribute application forms
  • Develop objective scoring rubric
  • Manage submissions confidentially
  • Facilitate blind judging process

  • TCO representative
  • External experts (1-2): University faculty or WHO/partner officials

Ensuring Fairness & Transparency

  • Blind Review: Initial judging done without knowledge of which department submitted
  • Conflict of Interest: Judges recuse themselves from scoring their own department's projects
  • Multiple Judges: Each submission reviewed by at least 3 independent judges
  • Score Averaging: Final scores calculated as average of all judges (excluding outliers)
  • Documentation: All scores and deliberations documented for accountability
  • Appeals Process: Departments may submit questions (not appeals) about process to Steering Committee
